I got an urgent message

Understand why this message is so important to take notice of

While ServerMate will warn you about this threat, it will not remove the fake/hoax bot and must be done manually

So, a few months ago; our main discord server had many - lets just say - angerd inderviduals telling us that they had been DM advertised to by the official ServerMate discord bot, which (of course) was not the case. They were sent a DM advertisement by another bot that was named exactly the same as our ServerMate bot and had the exact same icon, clearly these bots were purposely made to immitate our bot. But one of the worst things about this is that soon after you invite this fake bot, it will send a DM advertisement to all of your server's members telling them to also get the bot and after a certain amount of time, the owners of the fake bot will call a command that will make the bot DM advertise their own server to gain members. Which is just a disgusting practice in preying off of human vulnerabilities.


So, thus we have added a new feature to warn all of our current users about this issue. If your server has been found to contain a potentially misleading discord bot (which will only be fake ServerMate bots for now) the bot will come fourth to warn the owner of the server. While we understand that this will not make a huge difference (as most people who have the official ServerMate bot would least likely fall for the fake bot), I still believe that this could be a very helpful safegaurd for those confused when the recieve the DM, so then invite the fake bot.
